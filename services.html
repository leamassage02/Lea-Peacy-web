Our Treatment Menu

All our treatments are crafted around your comfort and health, combining skilled bodywork with organic botanicals to guide your body into deep restoration.

Swedish Massage

$200

A gentle full-body therapy using long, flowing strokes and soft kneading to melt tension and quiet the mind. Perfect for first-time visitors or a beloved ritual for returning guests, this treatment guides your body into deep, restorative calm.

Deep Tissue Massage

$300

Ideal for athletes, desk workers, and anyone carrying deep-seated tension, this treatment applies slow, deliberate pressure to reach the innermost muscle layers. Your therapist targets problem areas like the neck, lower back, and shoulders, releasing chronic soreness and restoring your full range of motion.

Hot Stone Massage

$139

Heated basalt stones are placed along key energy points and glided across your muscles, creating a warming sensation that penetrates deep beneath the skin. The radiant heat loosens tight muscles and eases stiffness, leaving you feeling balanced, grounded, and completely at peace.

Aromatherapy

$239

Combine the physical healing of massage with the therapeutic benefits of organic essential oils. Your therapist selects a custom botanical blend based on your current physical and emotional needs, absorbing the oils into your skin to clear airways, soothe tissues, and lift your mood.

Couples Massage

$350

Share a peaceful escape side-by-side in our private couples suite. Perfect for partners, close friends, or relatives, each person receives a fully customized massage (Swedish or Deep Tissue) from their own dedicated master therapist, sharing a quiet moment of absolute calm together.

Foot Reflexology

$120

Based on ancient meridian principles, this targeted therapy applies firm, precise pressure to specific zones on your feet that correspond to key organs and systems. This releases blocked energy, improves circulation, and creates a deeply relaxing sensation throughout your body.

Spa Enhancements

$40

Elevate any treatment with our premium add-on therapies. Options include a hydrating coconut oil scalp treatment, a mineral-rich hot towel back scrub, or target point stones. Designed to integrate seamlessly without adding extra time to your session.

Need Guidance?

If you are unsure which treatment is right for you, or if you have specific physical injuries or conditions, please contact our front desk. Our team of experienced master therapists will discuss your goals and help you select the ideal treatment plan.
